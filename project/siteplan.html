Site-Plan

Site Name

Name: Survival72

Survival72 represents a site designed to educate about the importance of emergency preparedness, emphasizing the need for a 72-hour survival kit. This name was chosen because it clearly conveys the main focus of the site on survival during the critical first three days of an emergency, when external resources may not be available.

Domain: Survival72.com

Site Purpose

The website project focuses on emergency preparedness for natural disasters, including floods, earthquakes, hurricanes, wildfires, tornadoes, and landslides. The site will emphasize the importance of having a 72-hour survival kit and provide comprehensive information to help individuals and families prepare for various emergencies.

Scenarios

  • How can I prepare my family for an earthquake?
  • What should a 72-hour survival kit contain?
  • How can I create an effective evacuation plan for my home?
  • What should I do in case of a medical emergency during a natural disaster?
  • Where can I find shelters and assistance in my area after a disaster?

Color Schema

Blue (#0056b3) Usage: Headings (h1, h2, h3), buttons of call-to-action, and links. Reason: Blue conveys trust, calmness, and security, essential for a site focused on emergency preparedness.

link

Orange (#ff9800) Usage: Buttons of call-to-action and accents. Reason: Orange attracts attention and indicates alertness, making it suitable for buttons and important notices.
