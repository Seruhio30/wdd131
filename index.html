Costa Rica

Costa Rica, a Central American country known for its rich biodiversity and stunning landscapes, is a haven for nature enthusiasts. It boasts beautiful beaches along the Pacific and Caribbean coasts, lush rainforests, and an impressive array of wildlife, including sloths, toucans, and howler monkeys. Costa Rica is celebrated for its commitment to environmental sustainability, with extensive national parks and protected areas. The country's "Pura Vida" lifestyle, emphasizing a laid-back and positive attitude, reflects the friendly and welcoming nature of its people.
